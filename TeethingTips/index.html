Teething Tips

Gentle advice for comforting your baby through their teething journey.

🍼Offer something to chew on

Cold teething rings or clean damp washcloths help soothe sore gums.

🖐️Massage their gums

Use a clean finger to gently rub your baby's gums to relieve pressure.

🎈Keep them distracted

Simple play or cuddling can offer emotional comfort and help distract from the discomfort.

🌿Use Rainbow Baby gel or powder

Our gentle, traditionally formulated products are made to calm and soothe teething symptoms naturally.

🧊 Use cold foods (if age-appropriate)

If your baby is eating solids, cold purees or yogurt can provide soothing relief to sore gums.

💧Wipe away drool

Teething often causes drooling. Keep your baby's chin dry to prevent irritation.
