Our Journey

Rainbow Baby began with one mission in mind: to bring gentle, natural relief to little ones and peace of mind to parents. Founded by a passionate caregiver and health enthusiast, our products are inspired by time-tested anthroposophic and homeopathic principles.

Each formula is carefully created to soothe teething discomfort while being safe and effective. As parents ourselves, we know how important trust is — and that’s why we’re committed to quality, transparency, and heartfelt care in everything we do.
